About us

Coinffeine is backed by a talented group of software developers whose broad interests about technology, distributed systems and economy brought them to the cryptocurrencies world. In late 2013, they put together all the concepts needed to design the next generation of exchange technologies.

We are located in Spain, where we have already worked together in projects on the big data field but our vocation and intentions are worldwide. From Spain to the blockchain!
